Learning Platform

It's an online learning platform available on Playstore. The app includes structured content in form of videos, audios, photos, pdfs, quiz. For the better monitoring of the users, it also includes a admin panel with analytics of all the users. The admin panel is for uploading the content and managing the users. For avoiding the fake users, the app is provided with phone number authentication using the OTP. The Events and News can be posted in the separate section for the group of users. This app aims to provide the easy access of educational material to every student in this tough times of covid.

  • Technologies used - Java, Android Studio, Firebase
  • Role - Android Developers

About us

We are a bunch of avid-developers, crazy to work on projects and enhance our skillset. It is a diverse group which is held firmly by this common belief-"To provide help to expect help". We have people from various domains spanning from development and machine-learning all the way to competitive-coders. If you are someone who has an idea but lacking expertise to put things into perspective, then you are at right place. We would love to join hands with you.

Our Mission

We endeavour to bridge the gap between the onset of an idea and its real-time execution. Young enthusiastic coders are usually thrilled by an idea but find a tough time implementing it. Our community boasts of experts who can bolster the idea and turn into a success by mutually working towards its attainment.

Our Vision

We see ourselves as a growing community building top-notch projects and side-by-side conducting informative sessions to upskill our members. We believe that people who have found a niche in this industry, should be encouraged to conduct sessions to make an atmosphere conducive to willing learners.
